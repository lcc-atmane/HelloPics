## App Features

**HelloPics** is an easy to use photo editing app that gives users full control over their images through a wide range of creative and functional tools.

### Available Tools

- **Crop** â€“ Trim away unwanted parts of the image.  
- **Select** â€“ Highlight and work with specific areas.  
- **Perspective** â€“ Correct the angle or viewpoint of your image.  
- **Perspective Crop** â€“ Crop while fixing distorted perspectives.  
- **Rotate & Flip** â€“ Rotate or flip your image in any direction.  
- **Free Cut & Crop** â€“ Cut out any shape freely by hand.  
- **Clone** â€“ Copy and blend parts of the image to other areas.  
- **Adjustment** â€“ Modify brightness, contrast, hue, saturation, and more.  
- **Add Text** â€“ Write and customize text over your photos.  
- **Add Image** â€“ Insert another image on top of your current one.  
- **Borders** â€“ Add borders or frames to your image.  
- **Resize** â€“ Change the image dimensions manually.  
- **Draw** â€“ Freely draw or sketch on your photo with brushes.  
- **Gradient Filters** â€“ Apply smooth gradient overlays for stylish effects.
